Unleashing Innovation
Unleashing Innovation is the CommBank Group’s open idea collection and management platform.

The platform offers the following three products to the Group’s Business Units, Support Units & Subsidiaries:

  • Time-bound innovation challenges
  • Continuous innovation channels
  • Continuous improvement logs

The objective of the platform is to:

  • Enhance our Group wide idea management capabilities by scaling the collection, connection, tracking and management of ideas
  • Empower our employees by creating a singular destination for idea submission
  • Create a consistent and connected innovation and ideation ecosystem
Coaching, Training & Facilitation
Innovation Capability Coaching supports and empowers our people to explore and solve their business problem/opportunities.

The coaching team leverages the Design Thinking and Experimentation mindset and tools to provide you with:

  • Coaching support for your initiatives
  • Workshop facilitation
  • Training on Design Thinking and Experimentation

The objective of Innovation Coaching is to:

  • Validate problem / opportunity spaces
  • Identify deep customer needs
  • Be creative (outside the box thinking)
  • Validate desirability of solutions through prototyping and testing
  • Train individuals and teams to leverage Design Thinking and Experimentation mindset and tools
Rapid Prototyping & App Development
Rapid Prototyping is about bringing ideas and concepts to life through an iterative approach to test and validate with real users.

The Rapid Prototyping team offers the following services:

  • Creation of storyboards and user journey maps
  • Low or high fidelity wireframes and clickable web/mobile app prototypes
  • Immersive experiences (Virtual, Augmented or Mixed Reality)
  • Web or Mobile Application Development

The objective of the Rapid Prototyping is to:

  • Empower teams to articulate ideas, test high-risk assumptions and gather insights
  • Provide quick iterations of designs to incorporate feedback from user testing
  • Equip teams with visualisations of their ideas to then pitch to stakeholders
  • Advise teams on best practices to enhance user experience
